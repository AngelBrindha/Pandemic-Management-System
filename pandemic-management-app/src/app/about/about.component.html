About the PANDEMIC HUB

Launched in February 2020, PANDEMIC HUB is a National public service campaign designed to educate and empower the public to prepare for, respond to and mitigate emergencies, including natural and man-made disasters. The goal of the campaign is to promote preparedness through public involvement.

Emotional Support

Avoid talking about the virus with people who tend to be negative or who reinforce and ramp up your fears. Turn to the people in your life who are thoughtful, level-headed, and good listeners. If you don't have someone you trust to turn to, apps such as 7 Cups are a good resource for free, emotional support.
