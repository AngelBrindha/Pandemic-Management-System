    50 Quotes to Help You Stay Strong in Difficult Times

    Written by:
    Chloe West

    Apr 08, 2020

    Life has never been more uncertain than it is right now, and with the COVID–19 pandemic hitting nearly every corner of the world, it can feel like the uncertainty will never end.

    We wanted to do our part in helping our users and everyone affected by putting together a list of 50 quotes or mantras to help us stay strong in difficult times and know that there will be a light at the end of the tunnel.

    On days you’re feeling down, find a few quotes you love within this list and save them. Turn them into phone or desktop wallpapers to look at often, or share them on social media to help others stay strong too.
